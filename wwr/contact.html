Meet the Team

Brenda Bochman

Brenda is our champion adventure leader. 29 years old and full of life, she never misses an opportunity to take the waves at full throttle. Adrenaline-junkie extraordinaire, Brenda knows how to handle the water when it's calm, but more importantly she knows how to have a heck of a lot of fun when the waves come crashing.

Lenny Lordando

26 years in the industry has taught Lenny one thing: life is full of risk. To him, it's all about picking the most fun risks and making the most of every situation. Lenny has been safety trained with the greats, like Henry Gorgin and even Helen Gravado, and he prides himself on being one of the first white water rafters to make it into the Guiness Book of World Records for rafting down a current blindfolded.

Frederick "Footless Freddy" Flunedrin

Footless Freddy is our leader in family fun on the rapids. Freddy leads our more casual groups of rafters, despite being one of our strongest team members. With his calm demeanour and humour to entertain kids and adults alike, you'll forget your fears and feel at ease on any wave.
