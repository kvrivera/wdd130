Whitewater Rafting Co.

Here at Whitewater Rafting Co., we believe that life is worth living BIG. We are passionate leaders and inspired risk-takers. We understand nature and claim her wonders as a muse for adventure. We see obstacles as something exciting, and overcomeable. Let us show you our way of life. We have a feeling you're going to like it.

History

Beginning as weekend adventure outings with friends, Whitewater Rafting Co. started small and very quickly built momentum. What started as a couple of friends hitting the rapids every weekend quickly turned into paid expeditions for adventure-seeking individuals and groups.

These were those who wanted professional guidance as they embarked on one of the most exciting and adrenaline-pumping experiences of their entire lives. We are proud to show our clients the wild side of the rivers they have walked and known their whole lives, and introduce them to what adventure truly feels like.
